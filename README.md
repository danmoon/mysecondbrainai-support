## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I record a voice note?**
Tap the microphone button on the main screen. 
AI will transcribe and summarize it automatically.

**How do I access The Vault?**
The Vault requires Face ID or Touch ID. 
Go to Rooms → The Vault.

**How do I restore my purchases?**
Go to Settings → Restore Purchases.

**My transcription isn't working — what do I do?**
Make sure microphone permission is granted in 
iPhone Settings → My Second Brain AI → Microphone.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

## Privacy Policy
Your audio and notes are processed on-device.
