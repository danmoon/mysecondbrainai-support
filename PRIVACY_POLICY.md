# Privacy Policy — My Second Brain AI

Last updated: April 22, 2026

## Overview
My Second Brain AI ("the app") is built with privacy as a core principle. 
Most processing happens on your device. We collect only what is necessary 
to provide the service.

## Data We Collect

### Account Data
- Email address and password (used for authentication via Supabase)
- Anonymous session ID before sign-up

### Voice & Audio Data
- Audio recordings you create are transcribed on-device using Whisper
- Transcripts and AI summaries may be sent to cloud AI services for 
  processing (Gemini, Groq)
- Audio files are stored locally and deleted after transcription

### Documents & Notes
- Documents you scan or import are stored locally on your device
- Notes and memories are synced to your private Supabase cloud storage

### Purchase Data
- Subscription status is managed by RevenueCat
- We do not store your payment information — all payments are handled 
  by Apple

### Usage Data
- Basic app usage analytics to improve the app
- No advertising identifiers are used

## Data Storage
- Local data is stored using ObjectBox on your device
- Cloud data is stored in Supabase with encryption in transit (HTTPS)
- Vault documents are protected by Face ID / Touch ID biometrics

## Third-Party Services
| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| Supabase | Cloud sync & auth | supabase.com/privacy |
| RevenueCat | Subscription management | revenuecat.com/privacy |
| Google Gemini | AI summaries | policies.google.com/privacy |
| Groq | AI transcription | groq.com/privacy |

## Your Rights
- **Delete your data:** Contact us and we will delete your account 
  and all associated data within 30 days
- **Export your data:** Contact us to request a copy of your data
- **Opt out:** You can use the app in offline mode to prevent 
  any cloud sync

## Children's Privacy
This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly 
collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We will notify users of significant changes via the app or email. 
Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
